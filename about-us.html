We are the expert Glass Architects.


The best glass architecture solutions in Kerala.


G- Concepts Solutions is one of the leading and authorized glass architectural companies with over 20 years of experience in Kannur (Kerala). The spectrum of applications for our glass is essentially limitless due to its aesthetic, technological, and energy-efficient qualities, which range from exterior glazing to industrial purposes. The range of alternatives available will allow architects and interior designers to select a material that helps them realize their innovative ideas and controls light.

We provide a wide variety of toughened glass end products in Kerala, including frameless toughened glass partitions and doors, glass works for projects, stainless steel and oak handrails with glass, structural glazing etc. Additionally, there are services like internal structure upkeep, constructing sliding and operable windows, thermal insulation, and sound abatement, etc.

To assure the quality of the work and timely completion, our works have been managed properly and served by professionals.
